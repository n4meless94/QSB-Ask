## Integration Findings

### BLOCKER-01: Invite To Assigned Moderator/Speaker Access

Organiser invite creates an `event_members` row with `status: "invited"` and `invited_email`, but access checks require an active row with `user_id`. No activation, acceptance, or linking path connects the invited email to a signed-in user.

Affected requirements: `AUTH-05`, `AUTH-06`, `AUTH-07`, and indirectly moderator/presenter flows such as `QNA-04` through `QNA-09`, `LIVE-03`, and `LIVE-04`.

Evidence:

- `src/lib/events/access.ts` checks active membership.
- `src/lib/events/access.ts` creates invited rows.
- `src/components/events/EventAccessPanel.tsx` describes manual onboarding, but no activation path completes it.

### BLOCKER-02: Participant Join To Survey Submission

Participant join redirects to Q&A, and the Q&A page renders only question submission/list UI. The survey page works at `/join/{code}/surveys`, but normal joined participants are not routed or linked there.

Affected requirements: `EVNT-03`, `EVNT-06`, `SURV-08`, `SURV-09`.

Evidence:

- `src/app/join/actions.ts` redirects to Q&A.
- `src/app/join/[joinCode]/qna/page.tsx` renders Q&A only.
- `src/app/join/[joinCode]/surveys/page.tsx` contains the implemented survey flow.
