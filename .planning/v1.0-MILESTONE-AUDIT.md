**Closed in Phase 04.1.** Organiser invite creates an `event_members` row with `status: "invited"` and `invited_email`. Server-side access checks now claim a matching invitation when the signed-in Supabase Auth user email matches the invitation, upsert the public profile, and activate one matching membership row by id.

Affected requirements: `AUTH-05`, `AUTH-06`, `AUTH-07`, and indirectly moderator/presenter flows such as `QNA-04` through `QNA-09`, `LIVE-03`, and `LIVE-04`.

Closure evidence:

- `src/lib/events/access.ts` checks active membership.
- `src/lib/events/access.ts` creates invited rows.
- `src/lib/events/access.ts` claims matching invited rows before assigned surface access is denied.
- `src/components/events/EventAccessPanel.tsx` describes sign-in based activation without claiming email delivery.
- `tests/events/event-access.test.ts` covers matching speaker activation and multi-role invited email activation.

**Closed in Phase 04.1.** Participant join still redirects to Q&A, and the Q&A page now exposes a Surveys link for the same event. The survey page links back to Q&A.

Affected requirements: `EVNT-03`, `EVNT-06`, `SURV-08`, `SURV-09`.

Closure evidence:
