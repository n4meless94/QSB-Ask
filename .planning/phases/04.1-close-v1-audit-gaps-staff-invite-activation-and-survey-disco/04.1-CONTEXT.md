# Phase 04.1 Context: Close v1 audit gaps

**Created:** 2026-06-01
**Source:** v1 milestone lifecycle audit closure

## Audit Gaps

1. Staff invite activation: organisers could create invited moderator or speaker access rows, but there was no activation path that linked a later signed-in staff account to a matching invitation.
2. Participant survey discovery: participant surveys existed at `/join/{code}/surveys`, but the ordinary join flow sent participants to Q&A with no visible survey navigation.

## Scope

- Convert matching invited `event_members` rows to active staff membership during server-side event access checks.
- Keep the activation path constrained to the authenticated user email returned by Supabase Auth.
- Update invite copy so it describes activation through sign-in, not email delivery.
- Add participant navigation between Q&A and Surveys pages.
- Add focused unit and E2E coverage for both closure paths.

## Out Of Scope

- Email delivery for invitations.
- New participant landing pages or broad navigation redesign.
- New survey authoring, result, export, deployment, or UAT functionality.
